Wings
A Buffalo wing, in the cuisine of the United States, is an unbreaded chicken wing section (flat,drumett) that is generally deep-fried then coated or dipped in a sauce consisting of a vinegar-based cayenne pepper hot sauce and melted butter prior to serving.
French Fries
French fries are served hot, either soft or crispy, and are generally eaten as part of lunch or dinner or by themselves as a snack, and they commonly appear on the menus of diners, fast food restaurants, pubs, and bars. They are usually salted and, depending on the country.
Chicken Nuggets
A chicken nugget is a chicken product made from chicken meat that is breaded or battered, then deep-fried or baked.They become a popular fast food restaurant item, as well as commonly sold as a frozen product for home use.Chicken nuggets are generally regarded as a fatty, unhealthy food.
